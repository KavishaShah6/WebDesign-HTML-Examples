Cheesecake Recipe

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar

For the filling:

  • 4 packages (8 ounces each) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (163°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and sugar for the crust.
  3. Press the crust mixture into the bottom of a springform pan.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  5. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  6. Stir in sour cream until fully combined.
  7. Pour the cream cheese mixture over the crust in the springform pan.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for about 50-60 minutes or until the center is set.
  9. Let the cheesecake cool in the pan, then refrigerate for several hours or overnight.
  10. Once chilled, remove from the springform pan, slice, and serve.
